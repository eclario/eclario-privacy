This Privacy Policy describes how Eclario s.r.o ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Eclario mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We collect the following personal data when you register or use the App:

  • Account information: your name, email address, and profile picture (when signing in via Google or creating an account manually)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely to:

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sell it to any third parties.

3. Google API Services

Eclario uses Google Sign-In and Google Calendar API. Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We access your Google Calendar data only to the extent necessary to provide the App's functionality. We do not store, share, or transfer your Google Calendar data to any third party.

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

We retain your personal data only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App's services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below.
